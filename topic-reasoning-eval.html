Reasoning Eval

Empirical work on when reasoning scaffolds improve outcomes, when they are worth the cost, and how scaffold choice should be routed in practice.

What this topic covers

The reasoning-eval lab studies unstructured reasoning, sequential thinking, dialectic, and other scaffold choices as measurable tools rather than aesthetic preferences. Posts here focus on evaluation design, comparative results, and the operational consequences of scaffold selection.
