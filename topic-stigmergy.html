Stigmergy

Environmental coordination, durable coordination objects, and the attempt to make multi-agent collaboration reconstructible instead of conversationally fragile.

What this topic covers

The stigmergy lab explores how agents coordinate through shared state rather than constant direct communication. Posts here cover signals, tasks, claims, verification, history reconstruction, and the translation of coordination research into working code.
